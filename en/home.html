SHS is a modding team dedicated to creating high-quality mods for Arma Reforger. We focus on building immersive, realistic, and well-integrated mods that enhance the gameplay experience.

Our flagship project, SHS NV and Lights, is a comprehensive modding framework that brings realistic night vision, thermal imaging, and infrared lighting systems to the game. This mod demonstrates our commitment to:

SHS NV and Lights Features:

  • Advanced Night Vision Goggles (NVG) - Multiple realistic NVG models with brightness controls, gain adjustment, and authentic visual effects
  • Thermal Imaging Systems - Multiple thermal vision modes (White Hot, Black Hot, Red Hot) with realistic heat signatures
  • Infrared (IR) Lighting - Complete IR light system that automatically syncs with NVG activation
  • Modular Architecture - Built from the ground up to be compatible with other mods without dependencies
  • Zero-Dependency Integration - Automatic detection of external NVG systems for seamless cross-mod compatibility

As a team, we're committed to creating mods that not only stand on their own but also work seamlessly with the broader Arma Reforger modding community.

ğŸ¤ Our Mission: Cross-Mod Collaboration

At SHS, we believe that the best modding experience comes from collaboration, not competition. Our core philosophy is built on three pillars:

1. Zero-Dependency Integration

We've designed SHS to work seamlessly with any NVG or lighting mod without requiring them to add SHS as a dependency. Our automatic detection system finds compatible mods and integrates with them automatically. This means:

  • Mod makers don't need to modify their code
  • Players can mix and match mods freely
  • No dependency conflicts or load order issues

2. Supporting All Modders

Whether you're a solo developer working on your first mod or a large team with years of experience, we want to work with you. We provide:

  • Comprehensive documentation - Clear guides for integrating NVG and IR lights
  • Technical support - Help with compatibility issues and integration questions
  • Flexible architecture - Easy-to-follow interfaces that work with any mod structure
  • Community resources - Shared knowledge and best practices

3. Building a Unified Ecosystem

Our goal is to create a collaborative modding ecosystem where hundreds of modders - both small and large - can work together to create amazing experiences. We envision:

  • NVG mods that work together seamlessly
  • IR lights that respond to any NVG system
  • Thermal systems that integrate with all equipment
  • A community where mods enhance each other, not compete
