Grey Reign Systems (GRS) is a professional, community-driven mod development team specializing in modular equipment, systems, and frameworks for Arma Reforger. We focus on realism, performance, and long-term maintainability—without sacrificing usability.

Design Philosophy

Every GRS asset is designed with real gameplay constraints in mind. Nothing is added without purpose, and nothing stays broken.

  • Modular systems that scale across factions and playstyles
  • Performance-conscious scripting and asset design
  • Clear iteration based on real player feedback
  • Framework-first thinking for long-term expansion

About Grey Reign Systems

GRS was founded with the goal of raising the standard for community-made content. We operate like a small studio—using structured development, testing cycles, and open communication with our users.

Our mods cover apparel, vests, rigs, belts, bags, weapons, helmets, electronics, and shared core frameworks that other modders can integrate into their own projects.

How We Work

  • Community polls directly influence development priorities
  • Beta testing and public feedback drive iteration
  • Dependencies are minimized to improve accessibility
  • Issues are tracked, acknowledged, and resolved transparently
  • Systems are built to support both PvE and PvP environments
