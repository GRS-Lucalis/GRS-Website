Network Component

GRS_NVL_NetworkComponent handles all equipment synchronization:

  • Lives on character prefab for easy access
  • Handles all equipment RPCs centrally
  • Recursively searches character and weapons for equipment
  • Prevents duplicate RPC calls with suppression flags
  • Supports late-joining players via RplSave/RplLoad
